\subsection{Syntax}

In the grammar in this section any whitespace shall denote an arbitrary positive amount of whitespace.

\begin{grammar}
	<File> ::= <Definition>*

	<Definition> ::= <Function Definition>
		\alt <Type Definition>

	<Type Definition> ::=
\end{grammar}
